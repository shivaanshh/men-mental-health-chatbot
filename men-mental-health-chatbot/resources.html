Helpful Resources & Helplines

You are not alone. Here are some organizations and resources for more help and information:

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (USA):
    24/7 free crisis support for mental health emergencies. Dial 988 or visit the 988 Lifeline website.
  • NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness):
    Information and support for those experiencing mental health issues. Call 1-800-950-NAMI or see NAMI's guide for getting help.
  • Find a Helpline (Global):
    Search for mental health helplines worldwide by country. Visit findahelpline.com to find support in your region.
  • Man Therapy:
    A humorous yet helpful site aimed at men’s mental health. Learn coping strategies and find resources at mantherapy.org.
  • Planned Parenthood – Men’s Sexual Health:
    Trusted information on sexual health, STD testing, contraception, and more. Read articles or find a clinic at Planned Parenthood Men’s Health.
  • Mayo Clinic – Men's Health:
    Expert articles on men’s health topics (mental well-being, sexual health, etc.). Browse the Mayo Clinic Men’s Health section for reliable information.

Remember, reaching out is a sign of strength. If you need help, don’t hesitate to contact a professional.
In an emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.

These links are provided for convenience and information. MindEase is not affiliated with these organizations.
